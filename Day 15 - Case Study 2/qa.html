Q&A about Candela's Candle

Q.What are Candela's scented candles made of? A.Candela's scented candles are made from soy wax, hard palm wax and natural essential oils.
Q.What is Candela's casting method? A.Candela candles are molded by hand.
Q.How long is the shelf life of scented candles? A.Normally, scented candles made from natural wax will have a shelf life of 2 to 3 years if unopened. After opening the lid, the fragrance only lasts about 6 months to 1 year.
